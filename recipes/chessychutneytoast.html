Chessy chutney toast recipe

Need something sweet 'n' spicy in a hurry?

This cheesy toast, topped with onion chutney and mature British cheeses, is bound to please the crowd. Swap the boule for sourdough for extra tang.

Ingredients

  • 30g finely grated mature Cheddar
  • 30g finely grated Cheshire cheese
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • ⅛ tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ tsp English mustard
  • pinch cayenne pepper
  • 2 slices white stonebaked boule
  • 1 1/2 tsp red onion chutney

Method

  1. Mix the grated cheeses, milk, Worcestershire sauce, English mustard and cayenne pepper in a bowl.
  2. Toast the boule slices.
  3. Spread with red onion chutney and top with the cheese mix. Grill on medium for 4-5 mins until melted.
